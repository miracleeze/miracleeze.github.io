GoldCoast Chamber of Commerce (GCC) website will be to educate, inform, and advertise businesses. And to share their growth to the general public to help inspire others to invest in the economics of the country. The website purpose will be to promote trades, give update of current events, and share training opportunities available to business owners.


The secondary purpose will be to provide mentorship and collaborations between businesses and potential stakeholders. And also to provide research resources. More importantly to announce and advertise our online social media presence.


The Chamber represents the voice of the business community; which includes business owners, business stakeholders, start-ups, university student, public and private universities, trader association, and foreign investors.


Our vision is to provide leadership for the growth and prosperity of the business in Ghana.
As our motto, “Your business links, Our business”
