Target Audience

Our target audience are mostly community members, visitors, and people planning to relocate into our community. Both men, women, and young adult will benefit from using this website. But our main focus will college student, local farmers, and family planning on vacation in our community. We know young adult will benefit from accessing our website from their Smartphones, tablets, and laptop.
