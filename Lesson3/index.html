Site Purpose

The purpose of creating this website is to provide up to date information of the every important event happening in our community, from weather forecasts, daily breaking news, update on sport activities as it happens, and information on preferable days to hold community and family events. An information page where community members can learn fun facts about weather. BlueSky Day website will provide information for visitor visiting the community for the first time. Information like fast food Spot, relaxation and live music event center, and gaming center for family to have recreational activities.
